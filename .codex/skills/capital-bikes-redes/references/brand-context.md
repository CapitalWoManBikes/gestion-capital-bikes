# Capital Wo-Man Bikes Brand Context

## Identity

Public brand name: Capital Wo-Man Bikes.

Secondary short name: Capital Bikes.

Location: Bogota, Colombia.

Address: Calle 65a #72-44, Edificio Magenta, Local 6, Avenida Boyaca.

Suggested phrases:

- Pedaleas tu, el resto lo hacemos nosotros.
- No ruedes solo, Capital Wo-Man te respalda.
- Tu bici segura, ajustada y lista para rodar.
- Mas que un taller, un respaldo para tu bici.

## Audience

Current customers:

- Urban cyclists.
- Fixed-gear cyclists.
- Hobby cyclists.
- Road and MTB customers.
- People looking for accessories, maintenance, and customization.

Customers to attract:

- Medium and high range bikes.
- Road and MTB cyclists who value serious technical service.
- People willing to pay for well-done maintenance.
- Customers who value diagnosis, evidence, safety, and trust.
- Cyclists who want to customize their bike.

## Services

Services:

- Mantenimiento de seguridad.
- Mantenimiento PRO.
- Diagnostico tecnico.
- Ajuste de cambios.
- Ajuste de frenos.
- Lavado y lubricacion.
- Desarme y ensamble.
- Revision de torque.
- Revision de rodamientos.
- Cambio de guayas y corazas.
- Cambio de pastillas, zapatas, cadena, pacha, cintas, corazas, neumaticos y componentes.
- Armado de bicicletas.
- Personalizacion de bicicletas.
- Venta de repuestos, accesorios y productos de ciclismo.

Star services:

- Mantenimiento PRO.
- Diagnostico tecnico con evidencia.
- Personalizacion de bicicletas.
- Armado y ensamble.
- Mantenimiento para ruta, MTB y pinon fijo.
- Servicio tecnico enfocado en seguridad.

Do not overpromote:

- Very cheap services.
- Repairs without diagnosis.
- Exaggerated promises.
- Fast fixes that compromise quality.
- Aggressive discounts all the time.

## Tone

Sound:

- Close.
- Technical.
- Practical.
- Trustworthy.
- Direct.
- Cyclist-minded.
- Slightly premium.
- Young but professional.

Do not sound:

- Generic.
- Like any random store.
- Desperate to sell.
- Focused only on discounts.
- Too formal or robotic.

Use these concepts:

- Seguridad.
- Diagnostico.
- Mantenimiento.
- Confianza.
- Torque.
- Ajuste.
- Revision.
- Evidencia.
- Proceso.
- Rodar.
- Taller.
- Bici lista.
- Servicio tecnico.

Avoid:

- Baratisimo.
- El mas barato.
- Excessive gangazo language.
- Constant solo-por-hoy urgency.
- Asking for 5 stars when requesting reviews.
- Promising results that depend on the real condition of the bike.

## Commercial Rules

Prices:

- Product, combo, promotion, and defined-service prices may be public.
- Complex technical service prices should often depend on diagnosis.

Discounts:

- 20% and 30% discounts may be published.
- Conditions must be clear.
- The brand should not depend only on discounts.
